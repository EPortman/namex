Manage Your Name Request
Manage your existing Name Request at www.bcregistry.ca/namerequest. You will need your NR Number and Applicant phone number or email address to access your Name Request.
Here you will be able to:
  • Extend the expiry date of your NR(Name Requests must be used within 56 days unless extended)
  • Cancel your Name Request
  • View consent status(when applicable)
  • Edit applicant and contact information
  • Download copies of receipts, results and more
